47SOUL

Combining Dabke – the traditional Palestinian street music and dance – with deep electronic and analogue beats, mixed with sounds of the Middle East, 47Soul celebrate their own struggles for freedom and equality at festivals and on dance floors around the world.

MAHALIA

A storyteller at heart, Mahalia’s unassuming songwriting manages to express an instinctively innocent gift for melody with a mature complexity in composition and a natural affinity for imaginative narratives.

CLIVE STAFFORD SMITH

Clive is the founder and Director of Reprieve, a human rights organisation which provides free legal and investigative support to some of the world’s most vulnerable people: those facing execution, and those victimised by states’ abusive counter-terror policies – rendition, torture, extrajudicial imprisonment and killing.

KATE RUSBY

Inhabiting a lyric with unforced conviction – no matter how old or how modern – Kate has that rare ability to transport her audience, of touching them emotionally and making each tune live vividly within their experience and imagination.
