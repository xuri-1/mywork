CAMPLIGHT

Pre Pitch Tent Hire

In our continuing drive to be more sustainable in 2017 we are pleased to welcome Camplight as our provider of pre-pitched tents.

Our festival-goers are a tidy bunch, and in 2016 left our campsite pretty much immaculate…but not all festivals have such considerate guests. Camplight was set up as part of the solution to campsite waste, and all the tents they hire out are picked from those abandoned at UK events, professionally fixed and restored. They’ll also be providing a fixing service for your tent if you need it on site.

One of the ways Camplight tackles the waste problem is by providing such good value pre pitched that some people won’t even be tempted to go out and buy a cheap disposable tent anymore!

With prices starting at just £10 per night, there are tents in a variety of shapes and sizes, along with bedding, chairs, accessories and utility tents available to rent.
